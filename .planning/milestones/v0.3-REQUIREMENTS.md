## Outcome Notes

- Anthropic embeddings were treated as an explicit capability gap, not as a
  partial implementation.
- The local compose demo counts as the `v0.3` packaging target; Kubernetes
  remained out of scope.
- `REFSVC-12` closed with live OTLP probe evidence plus Tempo retention checks
  after fixing collector reachability and explicit error span statuses.
- The historical `v0.3` requirements footer claimed `65 total`, but the
  enumerated requirement IDs on disk total `76`. This archive uses the
  enumerated IDs as source of truth: `72` shipped with `v0.3`, `4` carried
  forward into the gated deprecation cycle.
